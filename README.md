# Pretium 🌍
Pretium is a payment platform that bridges the gap between the existing financial value chain and the blockchain ecosystem. We enable users to pay for various goods and services at merchant points, such as groceries, supermarkets, restaurants, and fuel/gas stations.

## Mission 💡

We are on a mission to blur the distinction between digital assets and existing payments rails (fiat currencies) 💸

## Tagline 

Unlocking the value.

## Features

- Utility payments 💙
- Bill settlements 💙

## Why Pretium?

- Cost-effective
- Seamless utility & bill settlements
- Instant payments
- Earn cashback through commissions
